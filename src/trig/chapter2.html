Chapter 2 | Trigonometric Functions

Reciprocal Identities

\begin{equation} \begin{split} \sin(x) &= \frac{1}{\csc(x)} \;\;\; & \csc(x) &= \frac{1}{\sin(x)} \\ \cos(x) &= \frac{1}{\sec(x)} \;\;\; & \sec(x) &= \frac{1}{\cos(x)} \\ \tan(x) &= \frac{1}{\cot(x)} \;\;\; & \cot(x) &= \frac{1}{\tan(x)} \\ \end{split} \end{equation}
